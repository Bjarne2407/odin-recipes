Sweet Potato Curry

The greatest curry in the world

If you want to try a new and healthy recipe, you should definetly try this Sweet Potato Curry.

Ingredients:

  • sweet potato
  • water
  • onion
  • garlic
  • red pepper
  • red lentils
  • curry
  • olive oil
  • cashew nuts

Steps:

  1. cut the onion and the garlic, put some oil in the pan and fry them
  2. cut the sweet potato and red pepper and add them to the pan
  3. wash the lentils and put them in the pan with some water
  4. wait until there is no water left in the pan and add the cashew nuts
