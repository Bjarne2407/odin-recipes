Porridge

The perfect Porridge for a good start in the day

If you want to start your day healthy and want to get some energy you have to try this awesome recipe.

Ingredients:

  • oats
  • water
  • banana
  • apple

Steps:

  1. put some oats in a pot and add the fivefold amount of water
  2. cook the oats until there is no more water left
  3. cut the banana and the apple and add both to the pot
  4. heat everything up and add the topping you like
  5. Enjoy your meal ;)
