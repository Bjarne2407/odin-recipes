Quinoa Bowl

The perfect Quinoa Bowl

If you want to eat healthy and like to eat something different than just salad, you have to try this awesome recipe.

Ingredients:

  • quinoa
  • water
  • onion
  • garlic
  • red pepper
  • broccoli
  • zucchini
  • olive oil
  • cashew nuts

Steps:

  1. cut the onion and the garlic, put some oil in the pan and fry them
  2. cut the zucchini, broccoli and red pepper and add them to the pan
  3. wash the quinoa and put it in the pan withe some water
  4. wait until there is no water left in the pan and add the cashew nuts
  5. Enjoy your meal ;)
